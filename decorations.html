decoration

1/3
We apply sustainable water-based inks to glass in a variety of colours and opacities, offering effects such as glossy, matte, soft touch, frost, cement, gradient, iridescent, etc. We can cover the entire bottle or selected parts by achieving sharp or smooth transfer lines.
See examples
Advantages of coating:
  • Can be applied for all shapes and sizes of bottles and jars
  • Provides a protective layer, increasing the overall durability of the bottle
  • Guarantees full coverage and a homogeneous finish
  • Allows resistance to chemical and mechanical manipulation
  • Compatible with other bottle design techniques such as labelling, screen printing, and hot stamping

decoration

2/3
Screen printing is one of the most popular decoration methods in the packaging industry. Can be applied on the surface of glass in one round of printing. For high-quality and unique designs, we use UV or ceramic inks.
See examples
Advantages of Screen printing:
  • High resistance to mechanical and chemical impacts
  • Compatible with oval, flat, cone, square shapes of bottles
  • Allows for 360° printing on bottles, regardless of their shape or size
  • Compatible with all bottle design techniques
  • Allows to achieve the haptic design elements

decoration

3/3
Hot stamping is a decoration method that uses metallic foil to apply on a surface of glass. The technique highlights accents and elevates the perceived value of products, imparting a distinct and luxurious touch.
See examples
Advantages of Hot stamping:
  • Can be applied to coated bottles or clear glass
  • Compatible with oval, flat, cone, square-shaped bottles
  • Allows for 360° printing on bottles, regardless of their shape or size
  • Compatible all bottle design techniques
  • Resistant to mechanical and chemical impacts
